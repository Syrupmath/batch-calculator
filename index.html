The

Batch Cocktail Calculator

by Jeffrey Morgenthaler

This simple tool converts your single-serving cocktail recipe into a larger batch of any size, either for a dedicated number of servings, or exactly enough to fill a specific-sized container.

Step 1: Enter Recipe Name

Please enter the name of your cocktail recipe.

Step 2: Add Ingredients

Add all of the ingredients for your cocktail, specifying the ingredient name, volume quantity, and unit. Click "Add Ingredient" to include more ingredients. You can also remove ingredients using the "Remove" button.

Step 3: Scale Recipe

Select whether you'd like to scale by the number of servings or by total volume. Enter the quantity and, if scaling by volume, select the desired unit.

Choose this option if you know how many servings you need.
Ideal for filling a specific container or batch size.

Step 4: Add Dilution (Optional)

Choose a dilution percentage that suits your cocktail. If none of the presets fit, you can enter a custom dilution percentage.

Best if you plan to shake or stir your drinks to order.
