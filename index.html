Batched Cocktail Calculator

by Jeffrey Morgenthaler

Step 1: Enter Your Recipe

Start by naming your cocktail recipe. Next, input the base ingredients of your recipe, adding any additional ingredients as necessary.

  • Ounces
  • Teaspoons
  • Dashes
  • Ounces
  • Teaspoons

Step 2: Determine Your Batch Size

Specify the batch size. You can either enter the desired number of servings or the total volume of the container you plan to use.

  • Ounces
  • Liters
  • Quarts
  • Gallons

Step 3: Choose Dilution

Select your preferred dilution level. Choose the appropriate dilution for stirred cocktails (typically spirit-driven drinks) or shaken cocktails (drinks containing juice or dairy). Alternatively, select zero dilution if you plan to shake or stir the drinks to order.

%

Step 4: Calculate Totals

Click “Calculate” to generate your batched cocktail recipe. The scaled recipe will be displayed using the same measurement system as your original recipe.
